About Me

Hey there! I'm Samuel Hills, a passionate software developer currently diving into the world of tech during my apprenticeship at UBS. My journey into this field started with a love for computers fostered by endless hours of gaming, which eventually evolved into a fascination with the intricate world of software development.

In my arsenal of programming languages, I wield Swift for crafting captivating iOS experiences and JavaScript for weaving interactive web environments and small games. One of my recent ventures includes crafting an apprenticeship search program tailored for Swiss apprenticeships and students, alongside a pet project of mine, a small social media app dubbed "ZÜRI".

What drives me in this dynamic realm is the perpetual pursuit of staying abreast with emerging technologies. There's something truly magical about conjuring spells of code and witnessing them seamlessly come to life.

My interests gravitate towards iOS app development and the art of website building, where I find endless opportunities for creativity and innovation.

At UBS, I'm on a mission to soak up as much knowledge as possible. My goal isn't just to excel in my professional endeavors, but to also empower my peers and harness these newfound skills in both my personal and professional life.

Beyond the screen, you'll often find me hitting the gym. It's not just about staying fit; it's about cultivating discipline—a trait that proves invaluable when diving into the depths of new technologies or embarking on learning adventures in my field.

My philosophy? It revolves around the symbiotic relationship between discipline and learning—where one nurtures the other, and vice versa.
